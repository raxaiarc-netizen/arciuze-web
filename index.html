02 — Mechanism

Three steps. One rule.
You have to be there.

  1. 01

    Pick your communities.

    Tell us what you're actually into — film, climbing, hardware, whatever. We match you to communities built around that, not follower counts.

    · film · climbing · hardware · electronic · poetry · run club · sailing
  2. 02

    Show up to events.

    Each community runs real-world gatherings. RSVP in-app. You become a member. Cheap or free, small or large — the point is you're actually there.

03 — Feed physics

Your feed is ranked by
who was in the room.

No magic. No black box. The fan-out pipeline scores every post at creation and re-scores it every thirty minutes. These are the only multipliers:

5.0×

Attended

They were there. You were there. Scans matched. This is the multiplier everything else measures against.

3.0×

Joined

RSVP'd to the same event, but the scans didn't match. Still high intent.

2.0×

Friend

Mutually accepted friend. Adds a further 1.5× boost — but only after both scans.

1.5×

Community

Shared community, no event overlap yet. Consider it a seed.

Formula priority = (1 + log(1 + likes·3 + comments·5))
× 0.5^(ageh / 12)
× affinity × friend × 1000 Half-life: 12 h. Re-scored every 30 min.
04 — Surface area

Three ways to be seen.
Per event. Per person. You choose.

Mode A

Public

Every member of the event sees your full profile. Posts, grid, bio — the whole thing.

  • Full profile visible
  • All posts surface in-event
  • DMs unlocked to attendees
Mode B

Private

Members see a minimal card. Only fellow attendees — people who scanned in — see your full feed.

  • Bio, avatar, name only
  • Full profile to attendees
  • DMs unlocked to attendees
Mode C

Ghost

Invisible to strangers. Only accepted friends see anything. For when you're just there to be there.

  • Hidden from non-friends
  • Friends see full profile
  • DMs restricted to friends
